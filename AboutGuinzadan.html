Naragsak ay agew tako amin!. Welcome to Guinzadan this place is located in Bauko Mountain Provine. There are 3 barangays in Guinzadan which are Guinzadan Sur, Guinzadan Central, and Guinzadan Norte. The language they used here is kankanaey, in Guinzadan we have a unique culture or tradition for instance og-ogbo which is called bayanian in tagalog. Og-ogbo means helping others without recieving any rewards or helping them as a kailyan etc this is a common practices in our province, og-ogbo can be seen when their is calamities, weddings, death anniversary, when your nieghbor died etc. example is when your one of nieghbor died people usaully go and help them like cut some trees or "manga-ew", serve cofees,food and other else if thier is a wedding people help prepare the ingredents or help them cook.
